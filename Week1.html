Difference betwween kitting asdn weaving

Knitting is the process wherein thread is made into cloth and other crafts. It consist of stiches or loops of the material consecutively run together.

Weavin, on the other hand, is the process in which two types of yarn or threads are intercaled together to form a fabric or cloth
